<p><p>The PET plastic bottles recycling market comprises two primary types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves physically breaking down plastic waste into smaller flakes or pellets, which are then reprocessed into new products. In contrast, chemical recycling utilizes chemical processes to decompose PET into its basic monomers, enabling the creation of high-quality recycled material. Both methods aim to reduce environmental impact and promote sustainability, though they differ in their processes and end-product quality.</p></p>
