<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) is an advanced power transmission technology that integrates optical fibers with conventional conductors, enabling simultaneous power and data transmission. The market for OPPC can be segmented into two main types: Central Tube Structure OPPC, which features optical fibers housed in a central tube for enhanced protection and performance; and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC, where optical fibers are stranded in layers around the conductor, allowing for flexibility and reducing weight while maintaining strength and efficiency.</p></p>
