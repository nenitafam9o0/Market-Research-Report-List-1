<p><p>The milk testing devices market comprises primarily two types: ultrasonic and infrared milk testing devices. Ultrasonic devices utilize sound waves to measure milk properties, offering a non-destructive method to assess quality and composition, including fat and protein content. Infrared milk testing devices, on the other hand, employ infrared light to analyze the milk's components, providing rapid and accurate results for fat, lactose, and protein levels. Both technologies are essential for ensuring milk quality in dairy production and processing.</p></p>
